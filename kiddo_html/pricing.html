Join Kiddo Adventures Academy

At Kiddo Adventures Academy, we are committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our admissions process is designed to ensure that each child's unique needs and abilities are understood and catered to from the very beginning.

Admissions Information

We are currently accepting applications for the upcoming academic year. Please note that spaces are limited, and early application is recommended. 'You are the light of the world' Matt 5 v 14.

Our Admissions Process

The admissions process at Kiddo Adventures Academy is straightforward and designed to help us get to know your child better. We believe in creating a partnership with parents to ensure the best educational experience for each child.

  • Schedule a school tour

Required Documents

To complete the application process, please prepare the following documents:

Document Type Description Required
Birth Certificate Original or certified copy Yes
Immunization Records Up-to-date immunization history Yes
Previous School Records Report cards, transcripts (if applicable) Yes (for transfers)
Medical Information Health forms, allergies, special needs Yes
Passport Photos Recent passport-sized photographs Yes (2 copies)

Tuition and Fees

Kiddo Adventures Academy is committed to providing quality education at reasonable rates. Our fee structure is designed to be inclusive of most educational activities and materials. Please contact our admissions office for the current fee schedule.

Financial Assistance

Limited financial assistance may be available for qualifying families. Please inquire with our admissions office for more information about our scholarship programs and payment plans.
