Enriching Activities for Holistic Development

At Kiddo Adventures Academy, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our clubs program is designed to nurture the diverse talents and interests of our students, providing them with opportunities to explore, create, and excel in various fields.

Each club is carefully structured to develop specific skills while promoting teamwork, leadership, and creative thinking. Our dedicated staff ensures that every child receives guidance and encouragement to reach their full potential in a safe, supportive environment. 'You are the light of the world' Matt 5 v 14.

Our Clubs

STEM Club

Our STEM Club provides hands-on learning experiences in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. Students engage in exciting projects that foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills essential for future success.

Arts & Crafts Club

Our Arts & Crafts Club nurtures creativity and self-expression through various artistic mediums. Students explore painting, drawing, sculpture, and mixed media projects while developing fine motor skills and aesthetic appreciation.

Read More

Coding Club

Our Coding Club introduces students to the world of programming in a fun and engaging way. Children learn the basics of coding logic, develop simple games and animations, and build foundational skills for the digital future.

Read More

Sports Club

Our Sports Club promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy competition through various sporting activities. Students develop coordination, discipline, and sportsmanship while enjoying soccer, basketball, athletics, and more.

Read More

Music & Drama Club

Our Music & Drama Club nurtures performing arts talents through singing, instrument playing, and theatrical performances. Students build confidence, public speaking skills, and creative expression while preparing for school concerts and plays.
